About Me

Developer · Security Enthusiast · Knowledge Engineer

Hi, I'm ChenSchenG — a developer who lives at the intersection of security engineering, full-stack web development, and knowledge management. I believe the best way to truly understand something is to build it from the ground up, break it apart, and see how every piece connects.

My journey started with a curiosity about how things work under the hood — from the cryptographic handshakes that secure our communications to the event loops that power modern web servers. That curiosity led me to build tools, write deep-dive articles, and maintain an interconnected knowledge vault that maps concepts across domains.

I'm particularly fascinated by authentication and authorization protocols (OAuth 2.0, OIDC, JWT), network security (SSRF, gateway architectures), and the Node.js ecosystem. On the frontend side, I work with React and modern JavaScript tooling. I also keep an eye on developments in AI, especially transformer architectures and their applications.
