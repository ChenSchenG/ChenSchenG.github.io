About Me

Developer · Security Enthusiast · Knowledge Engineer

Hi, I'm ChenSchenG — a developer at the intersection of security engineering, full-stack web development, and knowledge management. I believe the best way to truly understand something is to build it from the ground up, break it apart, and see how every piece connects.

My journey started with a curiosity about how things work under the hood — from cryptographic handshakes that secure our communications to the event loops that power modern web servers. That curiosity led me to build tools, write deep-dive articles, and maintain an interconnected knowledge vault that maps concepts across domains.

I'm particularly fascinated by authentication protocols (OAuth 2.0, OIDC, JWT), network security (SSRF, gateway architectures), and the Node.js ecosystem. On the frontend side, I work with React. I also keep an eye on AI developments, especially transformer architectures.
